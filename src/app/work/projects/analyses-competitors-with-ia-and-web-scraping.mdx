## Overview

A smart tool that analyzes competitors, identifies opportunities, and boosts your business. It uses AI and web scraping to gather data from various sources, providing insights into market trends, customer behavior, and competitor strategies. The platform offers a user-friendly interface for easy navigation and data visualization, making it accessible for businesses of all sizes.

## Key Features

- AI-powered competitor analysis
- Web scraping for data collection
- Real-time market trend insights
- User-friendly data visualization
- Customizable reports and dashboards

## Technologies Used

- React
- Node.js
- Next.js
- Express
- NeonDB
- Tailwind CSS
- Google Auth
- Stripe

## Challenges and Learnings

One of the main challenges was ensuring the accuracy and reliability of the data collected through web scraping. We had to implement robust error handling and data validation mechanisms to filter out irrelevant or incorrect information. Additionally, integrating AI models for competitor analysis required extensive training and fine-tuning to deliver actionable insights.

Through this process, we learned the importance of maintaining a balance between automation and manual oversight. While AI and web scraping can handle large volumes of data efficiently, human intervention is sometimes necessary to ensure the quality and relevance of the insights provided.

## Outcome

The final product has been well-received by users, with positive feedback on its ease of use and the depth of insights it provides. Businesses have reported improved decision-making capabilities and a better understanding of their competitive landscape. The platform has also opened up new revenue streams through subscription-based access to premium features and data analytics services.
